## The steps of Basque making  
*   220g cream cheese
*   100g suger
*   4 eggs
*   8-10g cake flour
*   170g whipped cream
*   4g corn starch
*   preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius
*   bake at 220 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes
