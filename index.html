Basque making

  • 220g cream cheese
  • 100g suger
  • 4 eggs
  • 8-10g cake flour
  • 170g whipped cream
  • 4g corn starch flour
  • preheat the oven to 220 degrees Celsius
  • bake at 220 degrees Celsius for 30 minutes
